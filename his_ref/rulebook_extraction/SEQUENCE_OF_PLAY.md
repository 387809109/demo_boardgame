## Phase Structure (Every Turn)

| # | Phase | Rules § | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Luther's 95 Theses Phase | 18.1 | **Turn 1 only**. Protestant plays Luther's 95 Theses Mandatory Event card |
| 2 | Card Draw Phase | 8.2 | Add debaters, reformers, leaders; add new cards to deck and shuffle; roll for riches (§20.4); deal cards |
| 3 | Diplomacy Phase | 9.1–9.6 | **Limited on Turn 1**. Negotiation Segment (9.1) → Peace Segment (9.3, not on final turn) → Ransom Segment (9.4) → Excommunication Segment (9.5) → War Segment (9.6) |
| 4 | Diet of Worms Phase | 18.1 | **Turn 1 only**. Hapsburg, Papacy, and Protestant play 1 card each and resolve Diet of Worms |
| 5 | Spring Deployment Phase | 10 | Powers move one formation of land units from capital to a controlled space |
| 6 | Action Phase | 11 | Powers take impulses in order: Ottoman → Hapsburg → England → France → Papacy → Protestant. A Military or Religious victory may end the game |
| 7 | Winter Phase | 19 | Remove loaned naval squadron markers & Renegade Leader; return naval units to closest port; leaders & land units return to fortified spaces (attrition possible); remove major power alliance markers; add 1 regular to each friendly-controlled capital; remove piracy markers; flip debaters to uncommitted side; resolve specific Mandatory Events (see table below) |
| 8 | New World Phase | 20 | Resolve Voyages of Exploration; resolve Voyages of Conquest |
| 9 | Victory Determination Phase | 23 | Check for winner. If none, advance turn marker and start a new turn |
