- **Found Jesuit University**: 3 CP

- **Excommunication**: Slots for up to 7 targets (reformers + rulers)
- **Mercenaries**: Can buy mercenaries (1 CP)

## 6. Protestant

### Ruler

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Name | Martin Luther |
| Battle Rating | — |
| Command Rating | — |
| Admin Rating | Save 2 cards |
| Card Bonus | No extra cards |
| Note | Also a debater and reformer (in Wittenberg) |

### Action Cost Table

| Cost | Action |
|---|---|
| 1 | Move formation in clear |
| 2 | Move formation over pass |
| 1 | Buy mercenary |
| 2 | Raise regular troop |
| 1 | Assault |
| 1 | Control unfortified space |
| 1 | Translate scripture |
| 2 | Publish treatise |
| 3 | Call theological debate |

> Note: Protestant has **no naval actions** (no Naval move, no Build naval squadron)

### Card Draw Rule

- **5 cards** if 4 or more Electorates are under Protestant political control (religious influence is irrelevant in this case)
- **4 cards** in all other situations

### New Testament Translation Track (CP Spent)

| At Start | +1 CP | +1 CP | +1 CP | +1 CP | +1 CP | +1 CP |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0 CP | 1 total | 2 total | 3 total | 4 total | 5 total | Take 6 rolls |

> After 6 total CP spent: take 6 Reformation attempt rolls. Then may begin Full Bible translation in this language.

### Full Bible Translation Track (CP Spent)

| At Start | +1 CP | +1 CP | +1 CP | +1 CP | +1 CP | +1 CP | +1 CP | +1 CP | +1 CP | +1 CP |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0 CP | 1 total | 2 total | 3 total | 4 total | 5 total | 6 total | 7 total | 8 total | 9 total | Take 6 + 1 rolls |

> After 10 total CP spent: take 6 Reformation attempt rolls + 1 bonus roll.

### Special VP

- **+2 VP for every Electorate** that is under both Protestant religious influence AND Protestant political control
- **+0 to +15 VP** as indicated by the Protestant Spaces Track

### Bonus VP

- Includes **1 VP per debate rating** of Papal debater **disgraced**

### Special Mechanics

- **Translation**: Translate scripture 1 CP per step; separate tracks for New Testament and Full Bible; each language zone has its own track
- **Publish Treatise**: 2 CP
- **Theological Debate**: 3 CP to call
- **Mercenaries**: Can buy mercenaries (1 CP)
- **No Navy**: Protestant cannot build or move naval units
